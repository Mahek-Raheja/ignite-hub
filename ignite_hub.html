What is Ignite Hub?

Every college has that one coder who can build apps overnight, a designer who can make posters in minutes, a speaker who can sell any idea, and a strategist who can plan it all out. But these talents usually stay hidden in different corners of the campus, never coming together.

The Student Skill Incubation Hub is our way out of this situation. It's a place where students with different skills can meet, collaborate and create something bigger than themselves — whether that's a project, a competition team, or even a startup. It's about turning individual talent into collective success, and giving students a chance to learn, earn, and grow together.

How it works

Find teammates

Post your skills or browse others. Match based on roles, timelines and goals.

Build together

Form project groups, host sprint nights, and use shared resources to ship faster.

Launch & grow

Compete in competitions, launch portfolios, or turn a project into a startup — with mentorship and seed support.

Why join?

Learn by doing

Practical experience through real projects beats passive learning — build a portfolio that matters.

Earn opportunities

Monetize skills via freelance gigs, competition prizes, or startup equity when ideas scale.
